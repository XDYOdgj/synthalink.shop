Stay Cool All Night with the OIGAE Cooling Blanket

Designed for those who struggle with overheating while sleeping or experience night sweats, the OIGAE cooling blanket offers a unique solution. Using advanced Japanese Q-Max 0.5 arc-chill fibers, it actively absorbs body heat, helping you maintain a comfortable temperature throughout the night. Whether you're catching up on rest or just relaxing, this blanket ensures you're never too hot.

Its ultra-soft and lightweight construction makes it perfect for use in your bedroom or any cozy space. You won't feel weighed down, and the breathable feel will keep you refreshed all night long. With a double-sided design, you can switch between a cooling summer model and a warm, snug option depending on the weather and your personal preference.

Simple to care for, the blanket only needs a gentle machine wash and air dry. This makes it an easy-to-maintain addition to your daily life, ensuring lasting comfort and durability. Available in 5 sizes and 7 colors, it's not only functional but also a great gift for any occasion.
